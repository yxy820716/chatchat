## Article 3, Receipt, Acceptance, and Warranty of the Product

1. The renewal service provided by Party B must meet the technical specifications agreed upon in the contract. Party A must accept the service according to the contract and must complete the acceptance within 3 days after verifying the renewal service. The acceptance criteria are based on the updates displayed on the corresponding brand's official website or our maintenance website. If Party A has any objections to the renewal service provided by Party B, Party A must notify Party B within 5 days from the date of placing the order for the renewal service. If no notification is given, it is deemed that Party A has accepted the service, and the maintenance provided by Party B fully complies with the terms of this contract.

2. After the expiration of the renewal service period, if Party A requires Party B to continue providing the renewal service, both parties shall sign a new renewal contract, and the specific fees will be determined separately.

3. If the product under this contract is software, once the software is unsealed, registered, or activated, it cannot be returned or exchanged, and it is deemed that the product has been accepted. Party B is not responsible for the warranty of the product.

## Article 4, Obligations of Party A

1. Timely notify Party B of any detailed information and details related to equipment failures ("failure information") and follow the guidance of Party B's engineers (and assist Party B's engineers) in diagnosing the failures.

2. Ensure that Party B can access the equipment in a timely and effective manner, and provide sufficient premises, testing environment, failure information, communication conditions, and facilities as required by Party B to enable Party B to effectively provide services to the end-user.

3. For the installation or service of the software systems involved in the service, it must be ensured that the software or system has been legally obtained and authorized for use by Party B; if any third party raises any claims, negotiations, investigations, or lawsuits regarding the legality of the software or system, Party A shall handle and bear the responsibility.

4. Before the engineer dispatched by Party B provides the service, Party A must retain sufficient backups of the software, database, and operation records contained in the equipment, and restore the data and install the application software on its own after Party B provides the service. Party B shall not be liable for any compensation arising from data loss. Any backup/recovery performed by Party B's engineer at the request of Party A is not within the scope of the service, and Party B shall not be liable for it.

5. If there are any potential dangers or health hazards to Party B's engineers at the location of Party A's equipment, Party A must notify Party B in advance and provide or recommend appropriate safety measures.

6. When deemed necessary, Party B will perform appropriate maintenance on Party A's equipment during the service validity period, and Party A must provide necessary cooperation.

## Article 5, Scope of Services

1. This service applies only to equipment purchased and used within the territory of the People's Republic of China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan regions), and Party B will provide this service only within the territory of China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan regions).

2. Unless otherwise agreed, the service is based on the brief description of the service product, this agreement, the statement of work, and Party B's introduction and/or explanation of the service. The scope of the service may include the repair of the equipment and the installation, inspection, and other services of the equipment.

3. The service is limited to the original factory configuration (as per the packing list accompanying the equipment) of computers and related products ("equipment"), and does not apply to any separately added accessories or any third-party products and accessories outside the brand of the equipment.

4. This service is independent of the service commitment attached to the equipment ("baseline service"); where this contract does not specify, the service will be subject to the relevant terms of the baseline service.

## Article 6, Exceptions to the Service

1. Consumable components (e.g., batteries), software product media, licenses, and text updates are not included in the service.

2. The service does not include necessary repairs and maintenance of the equipment due to the following reasons, including but not limited to:

- Party A did not install or use the equipment as required by the instructions;
- The equipment was installed, repaired, or disassembled by unauthorized service personnel;
- Accidental factors or human behavior (including computer viruses, operational errors, handling, bumps, inputting inappropriate voltage, etc.) caused the failure; unless otherwise specified in the service content;
- Normal wear and tear or damage caused by reasonable use (such as natural wear and tear of the casing, connectors, etc.);
- Party A took deceptive or improper means or methods to obtain benefits under this clause;
- Other force majeure events (such as earthquakes, fires, lightning, floods, etc.) causing equipment failure.

## Article 7, Liability for Breach of Contract

1. If Party A fails to make payment on time, a penalty of 5% of the overdue amount will be calculated for each day of delay; if the delay exceeds 15 days, Party B has the right to unilaterally terminate this contract, and Party B will no longer fulfill the unperformed renewal service obligations.

2. If Party B fails to provide the renewal service on time, a penalty of 5% of the total amount payable will be calculated for each day of delay, with the total penalty not exceeding 5% of the total contract amount.

3. If either party breaches the contract, all actual expenses (including but not limited to litigation fees, preservation fees, litigation property preservation guarantee fees, lawyer fees, travel expenses, appraisal fees, notarization fees, etc.) incurred by the non-breaching party to claim rights against the breaching party shall be borne by the breaching party.
