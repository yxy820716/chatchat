## Article 3, Acceptance, Inspection, and Warranty of Products

1. The renewal service provided by Party B meets the technical specifications agreed upon in the contract. Party A needs to accept the service as per the contract and must inspect the service within 3 days after verifying the renewal service. The acceptance criteria are based on the updates displayed on the corresponding brand's official website or our maintenance and warranty website. If Party A has any objections to the renewal service provided by Party B, Party A should notify Party B within 5 days from the date of placing the order for the renewal service. If no notification is given, it is deemed that Party A accepts the service, and the maintenance and warranty provided by Party B fully comply with the terms of this contract.

2. After the expiration of the renewal service period, if Party A requires Party B to continue providing the renewal service, both parties will sign a new renewal contract, and the specific fees will be separately agreed upon.

3. If the product under this contract is software, once the software is unsealed, registered, or activated, it cannot be returned or exchanged, and it is deemed that the product has been accepted. Party B is not responsible for the warranty of the product.

4. If Party A uses the software provided by Party B for further development, reproduction, decryption, decompilation, disassembly, or other reverse engineering, thereby infringing on the intellectual property rights of any third party or engaging in other acts of infringement, Party B shall bear no responsibility.

## Article 4, Obligations of Party A

1. Promptly notify Party B of any detailed information and details regarding equipment failures ("failure information") and follow the guidance of Party B's engineers (and assist Party B's engineers) in diagnosing the failure.

2. Ensure that Party B can promptly and effectively access the equipment and provide sufficient premises, testing environment, failure information, communication conditions, and facilities as required by Party B to enable Party B to effectively provide services to the end user.

3. For the installation or service of any software systems involved in the service process, ensure that the legal authorization and permission for such software or systems have been obtained and allow Party B to use them. If any third party raises any claims, negotiations, investigations, or lawsuits regarding the legality of such software or systems, Party A shall handle and bear the responsibility.

4. Party A must maintain sufficient backups of the software, databases, and operation records contained in the equipment before the engineer dispatched by Party B provides the service, and restore the data and install the application software on its own after the service is provided. Party B shall not be liable for any compensation due to data loss. Any backup/recovery actions performed by Party B's engineers at the request of Party A are not part of the service scope, and Party B shall not bear any responsibility for them.

5. If there are any potential dangers or health hazards to Party B's engineers at the location of Party A's equipment, Party A must notify Party B in advance and provide or recommend appropriate safety measures.

6. Party B may perform appropriate maintenance on Party A's equipment during the service period when deemed necessary, and Party A must provide the necessary cooperation.

## Article 5, Scope of Services

1. This service applies only to equipment purchased and used within the territory of the People's Republic of China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan regions), and Party B will provide this service only within the territory of China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan regions).

2. Unless otherwise agreed, the service is based on the brief description of the service product, this agreement, the statement of work, and Party B's introduction and/or explanation of the service. The scope of the service may include repair of the equipment, as well as installation, inspection services, etc.

3. The service is limited to the original factory configuration (as per the packing list included with the equipment) of computers and related products ("equipment"), and does not apply to any separately added accessories or any third-party products and accessories outside the brand of the equipment.

4. This service is independent of the service commitment that comes with the equipment ("baseline service"); for any terms not explicitly stipulated in this contract, the baseline service terms will apply.

## Article 6, Exceptions to the Service

1. Various consumable components (such as batteries), software product media, licenses, and text updates are not covered by the service.

2. The service does not include necessary repairs and maintenance of the equipment caused by the following reasons, including but not limited to:

- Party A did not install or use the equipment according to the instructions;
- The equipment was installed, repaired, or dismantled by unauthorized service personnel;
- Failures caused by accidental factors or human behavior (including computer viruses, operational errors, handling, bumps, inputting unsuitable voltage, etc.); except where otherwise specified in the service content;
- Wear and tear or damage caused by normal and reasonable use (such as natural wear and tear, wear, and aging of the casing and connectors);
- Party A took improper means or methods, such as concealment and fraud, to obtain the benefits under this clause;
- Equipment failures caused by other force majeure (such as earthquakes, fires, lightning, floods, etc.).

## Article 7, Liability for Breach of Contract

1. If Party A delays payment, a penalty of 0.5% of the unpaid amount per day will be charged; if the delay exceeds 15 days, Party B has the right to unilaterally terminate this contract, and Party B will no longer fulfill the unperformed renewal service obligations.

2. If Party B fails to provide the renewal service on time, a penalty of 5% of the total amount due per day will be charged, with the total penalty not exceeding 5% of the total contract amount.

3. If either party breaches the contract, all actual expenses incurred by the non-breaching party to protect its rights and interests (including but not limited to litigation fees, preservation fees, litigation property preservation guarantee fees, lawyer fees, travel expenses, appraisal fees, notarization fees, etc.) shall be borne by the breaching party.
