## Article 3, Acceptance, Inspection, and Warranty of the Product

1. The renewal service provided by Party B must meet the technical specifications agreed upon in the contract. Party A must inspect the service according to the contract, and Party A must complete the inspection within 3 days after verifying the renewal service. The inspection criteria are based on the latest updates displayed on the corresponding brand's official website or our maintenance website. If Party A has any objections to the renewal service provided by Party B, Party A should notify Party B within 5 days from the date of placing the order for the renewal service. If no notification is given, it is deemed that Party A has accepted the service, and the maintenance provided by Party B fully complies with the terms of this contract.

2. After the expiration of the renewal service period, if Party A requires Party B to continue providing the renewal service, both parties shall sign a new renewal contract, and the specific fees will be separately agreed upon.

3. If the product under this contract is software, once the software is unsealed, registered, or activated, it cannot be returned or exchanged, and it is deemed that the product has been accepted. Party B is not responsible for the warranty of the product.

4. If Party A uses the software provided by Party B for further development, reproduction, decryption, decompilation, disassembly, or other reverse engineering, thereby infringing on any third-party intellectual property rights or engaging in other acts of infringement, Party B shall bear no responsibility.

## Article 4, Obligations of Party A

1. Promptly notify Party B of any detailed information and details regarding equipment failures ("failure information") and follow the guidance of Party B's engineers (and assist Party B's engineers) in diagnosing the failure.

2. Ensure that Party B can access the equipment in a timely and effective manner, and provide sufficient premises, testing environment, failure information, communication conditions, and facilities as required by Party B to enable Party B to effectively provide services to the end-user.

3. For the installation or service of software systems involved in the service, Party A must ensure that it has legally obtained the authorization and license for such software or systems and allows Party B to use them. If any third party raises any claims, negotiations, investigations, or lawsuits regarding the legality of such software or systems, Party A shall handle and bear the responsibility.

4. Before the engineer dispatched by Party B provides the service, Party A must retain sufficient backups of the software, databases, and operational records contained in the equipment, and restore the data and install the application software on its own after the service is provided by Party B. Party B shall not bear any liability for compensation due to data loss. Any backup/recovery actions performed by Party B's engineers at the request of Party A are not part of the service scope, and Party B shall not be liable for them.

5. If there are any potential dangers or health hazards to Party B's engineers at the location of Party A's equipment, Party A must notify Party B in advance and provide or recommend the necessary safety measures.

6. Party B may perform appropriate maintenance on Party A's equipment during the service validity period as needed, and Party A must provide necessary cooperation.

## Article 5, Scope of Service

1. This service is only applicable to equipment purchased and used within the territory of the People's Republic of China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan regions), and Party B will only provide this service within the territory of China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan regions).

2. Unless otherwise agreed, the service is based on the brief description of the service product, this agreement, the statement of work, and Party B's introduction and/or explanation of the service. The scope of service may include repair of the equipment, as well as installation, inspection services, etc.

3. The service is limited to the original factory configuration (as per the packing list accompanying the equipment) of computers and related products ("equipment"), and does not apply to any separately added accessories or any third-party products and accessories outside the equipment brand.

4. This service is independent of the service commitment that comes with the equipment ("baseline service"); for any terms not explicitly specified in this contract, the baseline service terms will apply.

## Article 6, Exceptions to the Service

1. Various consumable components (e.g., batteries), software product media, licenses, and text updates are not included in the service.

2. The service does not include necessary repairs and maintenance of the equipment due to the following reasons, including but not limited to:

- Party A did not install or use the equipment according to the instructions;
- The equipment was installed, repaired, or disassembled by unauthorized service personnel;
- Failures caused by accidental factors or human behavior (including computer viruses, operational errors, handling, bumps, inputting inappropriate voltage, etc.); except where otherwise specified in the service content;
- Normal wear and tear or damage from reasonable use (such as natural wear and tear, wear, and aging of the casing and connectors);
- Party A took deceptive or improper means or methods to obtain benefits under this clause;
- Other force majeure events (such as earthquakes, fires, lightning, floods, etc.) causing equipment failures.

## Article 7, Liability for Breach of Contract

1. If Party A fails to make payment on time, a penalty of 5% of the unpaid amount will be calculated per day overdue; if the payment is overdue for more than 15 days, Party B has the right to unilaterally terminate this contract, and Party B will no longer fulfill the remaining renewal service obligations.

2. If Party B fails to provide the renewal service on time, a penalty of 5% of the total amount due will be calculated per day overdue, with the total penalty not exceeding 5% of the total contract amount.

3. If either party breaches the contract, the non-breaching party shall bear all actual costs (including but not limited to litigation fees, preservation fees, litigation property preservation guarantee fees, lawyer fees, travel expenses, appraisal fees, notarization fees, etc.) incurred in pursuing the breaching party to protect their rights.
